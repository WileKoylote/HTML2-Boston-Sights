Top 3 Animals In The Boston Aquarium

Ever wondered what a giant octopus, jelly fish, or sea horse looked up close? Well now you can!
The Boston Aquarium has all three and more! Here are the top 3 animals we got to see!

1. Giant Octopus

This rarely seen creature lives deep in the north atlantic and eats nearly any sea creature relative or smaller compared to it's size!

2. Leafy Sea Dragon

Another rare sight, the Leafy Sea Dragon lives in the Western and Southern parts of Australia and feeds on algae and and seaweed.

3. Moon Jellyfish

Found all over the ocean, this cute and glowing jellyfish is actually one of the few non-poisonus jelly fish to humans!
